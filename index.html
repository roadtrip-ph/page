Frequently Asked Questions

How can RoadTrip PH help me rent a car?
RoadTrip PH has a listing of vehicle rentals which can help you make an informed choice in deciding which rental service to used for your trip.

Aside from viewing RoadTrip PH listings, what other services can I avail of?
RoadTrip PH is also your personal transport concierge. We can also help you canvass for the most affordable alternative price available for your destination and book it for you.

For which of my plans can RoadTrip PH be of help?
From family vacations, to barkada trips, to company outings, to airport transfers, to spontaneous travels, we can help you with your plans!

Does RoadTrip PH own its rental vehicles?
No. RoadTrip PH hosts a registry of vehicle rental providers.

Do you rent vans/cars without drivers?
No. Our suppliers have their own drivers to get you from point A to point B.
